find

full text of the book is available as a printable download here or as a webpage here. if you’d prefer to have a handmade physical copy, the book is available for about $12 at under the umbrella bookstore . with support from community members and some scrappiness of our own we have done as much as possible to keep material costs low so we can distribute the book widely, to anyone who wants one, with no expectation of money coming to us. if you’d like a book for free or less than $12, fill out the google form linked below, or find us at most lake-facing events in slc.

25% of any profit we make on the book goes to Boa Ogoi, a Northern Shoshone cultural center on the banks of the Bear River. This indigenous-led project, which sits at the site of the bear river massacre, is dedicated to restoring the sacred, living landscapes that are precious to the native people of the salt lake basin.

another 25% goes to Crips for Esims for Gaza, a grassroots organization dedicated to supporting the Palestinian people and their struggle for liberation.

50% of our profits remain with the project to fund the ongoing material costs of making books.

you can read more about our material costs here.

if you’d like to order a copy online, please fill out our order form.
